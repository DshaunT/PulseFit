Get Stronger with PulseFit Workouts

Explore a variety of exercises to transform your body. We've categorized them into strength, endurance, and flexibility workouts, complete with suggested reps and benefits!

Strength Workouts

Squats

Benefits: Builds leg muscles, strengthens core, improves flexibility and mobility.

Suggested Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Deadlifts

Benefits: Targets back, hamstrings, and glutes, enhances posture and core strength.

Suggested Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps

Bench Press

Benefits: Strengthens chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Suggested Reps: 4 sets of 8-12 reps

Endurance Workouts

Running

Benefits: Boosts cardiovascular health, improves stamina and endurance.

Suggested Reps: Run 30 minutes, 3-4 times per week

Jump Rope

Benefits: Improves coordination, cardiovascular health, and burns calories quickly.

Suggested Reps: 3 sets of 3 minutes

Cycling

Benefits: Improves leg strength, enhances endurance, low-impact exercise.

Suggested Reps: 45 minutes, 3-5 times a week

Flexibility Workouts

Yoga

Benefits: Increases flexibility, reduces stress, improves posture.

Suggested Reps: 30-minute session, 3-4 times a week

Stretching

Benefits: Improves flexibility, reduces muscle tension, enhances blood flow.

Suggested Reps: 15-20 minutes of stretching post-workout

Pilates

Benefits: Improves flexibility, strengthens core, promotes overall balance.

Suggested Reps: 3-4 sessions per week, 30 minutes
