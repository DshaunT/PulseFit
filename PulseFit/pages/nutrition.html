Daily Nutritional Needs

Your daily caloric intake varies depending on activity level, age, and goals. Here's a general guideline:

Sedentary

~ 2,000 - 2,200 calories/day

Moderately Active

~ 2,300 - 2,500 calories/day

Active

~ 2,800 - 3,000 calories/day

Healthy Meal Suggestions

Chicken Salad

Benefits: Packed with lean protein and fiber, perfect for building muscle and aiding digestion.

Ingredients: Chicken breast, mixed greens, avocado, tomatoes, olive oil dressing.

Calories: ~350 calories per serving

Quinoa Bowl

Benefits: A great source of complex carbs and plant-based protein. Supports energy levels and muscle recovery.

Ingredients: Quinoa, black beans, corn, avocado, lime dressing.

Calories: ~450 calories per serving

Salmon & Veggies

Benefits: Rich in omega-3 fatty acids and vitamins, supports heart health and reduces inflammation.

Ingredients: Grilled salmon, asparagus, broccoli, olive oil.

Calories: ~500 calories per serving
